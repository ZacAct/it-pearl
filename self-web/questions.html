Q&A

What platforms is RDR2 available on?
The game is available on PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC. It is also playable on next-gen consoles like PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S through backward compatibility.
Can you play Red Dead Redemption 2 online?
Yes! Red Dead Online is the multiplayer component of the game, allowing players to explore the world of RDR2 with friends or other online players. It features cooperative missions, competitive modes, and various activities to enjoy together.
How does the honor system work?
The honor system tracks Arthur Morgan’s moral choices throughout the game. Positive actions, like helping strangers or sparing enemies, increase honor, leading to better interactions and discounts in stores. Negative actions, such as committing crimes or being ruthless, decrease honor and result in a more feared reputation.
Can you interact with NPCs?
One of RDR2’s standout features is its interactive NPC system. Players can greet, antagonize, rob, or help almost every character in the game. The choices made affect the world’s perception of Arthur and can lead to unique encounters and story moments.
How long does it take to complete the game?
The main story takes approximately 50–60 hours to complete, but achieving 100% completion, exploring the world, and engaging in side activities can push playtime well beyond 100 hours.
